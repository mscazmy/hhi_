Our Mission

To provide high-quality health professions education that empowers students with the scientific knowledge, clinical skills, professional values, and ethical grounding needed to serve the health needs of the people of Wasit Governorate and Iraq.

We are committed to academic excellence, continuous improvement, community engagement, and the integration of evidence-based practices into all aspects of our teaching and clinical training.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of health education in southern Iraq, recognized for producing graduates who are competent, compassionate, and committed to the advancement of healthcare in their communities.

History of the Institution

The Higher Health Institution – Wasit was established in the mid-1990s in response to a critical shortage of trained healthcare workers in the governorate. Initially offering a single nursing program, the institution expanded steadily to include Emergency Medicine and Midwifery departments, growing its faculty and student body year by year.

Over the decades, HHI Wasit has graduated hundreds of nurses, emergency technicians, and midwives who serve in hospitals, health centers, and communities across Wasit and beyond. The institution is affiliated with the Iraqi Ministry of Health and operates under the supervision of the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research.

Founding

Established in Al-Kut with the Nursing Department as the first academic program.

Expansion

Added Emergency Medicine and Midwifery departments to address regional health workforce needs.

Accreditation

Earned national accreditation from the Ministry of Higher Education for all three programs.

New Facilities

Opened modern simulation labs and skills centers to enhance clinical training quality.

Clinical Partnerships

HHI Wasit maintains formal agreements with the following institutions for student clinical placements:

Al-Kut Teaching Hospital

Primary clinical site for Nursing and Emergency students. Full ward access for supervised training.

Wasit Emergency Center

Specialized clinical placement for Emergency Medicine students, with access to trauma and resuscitation cases.

Wasit Maternity Hospital

Dedicated maternity and gynecology placements for Midwifery students under senior midwife supervision.

District Health Centers

Primary healthcare and community health placements across multiple districts of Wasit Governorate.
